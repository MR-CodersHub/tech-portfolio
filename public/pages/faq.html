Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my services, process, and collaboration

General

What services do you offer?
I offer full-stack development, system architecture design, technical consulting, DevOps & CI/CD setup, code review and optimization, and mentorship & training. Each service is tailored to meet your specific needs.
What technologies do you work with?
I specialize in React, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, AWS, Docker, Kubernetes, and modern DevOps tools. I'm always learning new technologies to provide the best solutions for each project.
Do you work with startups or only established companies?
I work with both! I've helped startups build their MVP and scale to thousands of users, as well as assisted established companies in modernizing their systems and improving performance.

Projects & Pricing

How do you charge for your services?
I offer three pricing models: hourly consulting ($150/hour), project-based fixed pricing, and monthly retainers ($8,000/month). The best option depends on your project scope and timeline. Contact me for a custom quote.
How long does a typical project take?
Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple web application might take 4-6 weeks, while complex enterprise systems can take several months. I provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation.
Do you require a deposit?
For project-based work, I typically require a 50% deposit to begin, with the remainder due upon completion. For retainer agreements, payment is made monthly in advance.
What's included in the project cost?
Project costs include development, testing, deployment, documentation, and 30 days of post-launch support. You'll own all source code and have full rights to the work produced.

Process & Communication

What's your development process?
I follow an agile approach with regular check-ins and iterative development. You'll receive weekly progress updates, and we'll have milestone reviews to ensure the project stays on track and meets your expectations.
How do we communicate during the project?
I'm flexible with communication channels. We can use email, Slack, Microsoft Teams, or video calls (Zoom, Google Meet) based on your preference. I provide regular updates and am responsive to questions.
Do you sign NDAs?
Yes, I'm happy to sign NDAs and other confidentiality agreements to protect your intellectual property and business information.

Support & Maintenance

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?
Yes! All projects include 30 days of post-launch support. After that, I offer maintenance packages for ongoing updates, bug fixes, and feature additions.
What if I need changes after the project is complete?
Minor adjustments during the 30-day support period are included. For larger changes or new features, we can discuss hourly rates or a new project scope.
Do you provide training for my team?
Absolutely! I can provide training sessions to help your team understand and maintain the system. This can be included in the project scope or arranged separately.

Getting Started

How do I get started?
Simply reach out through the contact form or email me at workharishragav@gmail.com. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your project, timeline, and budget.
What information should I prepare for our first call?
Have a clear idea of your project goals, target audience, desired features, timeline, and budget. Any existing documentation, wireframes, or design assets are helpful but not required.
Are you currently accepting new projects?
Yes! I'm always open to discussing new opportunities. Depending on my current workload, there may be a short wait time before starting, but I'll let you know upfront during our initial consultation.

Still Have Questions?

Don't see your question here? Feel free to reach out, and I'll be happy to help!
